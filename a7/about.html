Paul, Penny and their children want you to know that they cherish the land they work. For nearly a hundred years, this farm has been in the Piper family and they intend to keep it way. Over the years, the world has changed, but here on the Piper farm, they continue to do things the same way they have since grandpa Peter plowed these fields. And they think it shows in their product.

All of our peppers are grown 100% organic. Just like grandpa Peter grew them years ago.
